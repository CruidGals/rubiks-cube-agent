# Keybinds

Listed below are the keybinds for this Rubik's Cube simulator. You may notice that these binds follow the standard for Rubik's Cube notation. Extended functionality to change keybinds will be added in the future.

Holding the `shift` key while moving a layer will turn the desired layer in the opposite direction. Holding the `ctrl` while moving a layer will perform a "wide" move.

## Regular Moves

- `R`: Right Layer
- `L`: Left Layer
- `F`: Front Layer
- `B`: Back Layer
- `U`: Top Layer
- `D`: Bottom Layer

## Other Moves

- `M`: Middle Layer (parallel to right/left layer)
- `S`: Middle Layer (parallel to front/back layer)
- `E`: Middle Layer (parallel to top/bottom layer)
- `x`: Turn the cube in the direction of the right layer
- `y`: Turn the cube in the direction of the top layer
- `z`: Turn the cube in the direction of the front layer
